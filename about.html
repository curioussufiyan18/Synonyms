How It Started

We saw a gap.
We filled it.

Too many great brands were getting cookie-cutter strategies from agencies that didn't take the time to understand them. We started Synonym Social to fix that.

We built an agency rooted in social-first thinking, premium creative, and strategy that's actually customized — not templated. Every campaign, every caption, every pixel is intentional.

Based in Toronto, we work with restaurants, fashion brands, wellness companies, and modern consumer businesses who want to grow with purpose.

"We don't just market your brand — we become fluent in it."
